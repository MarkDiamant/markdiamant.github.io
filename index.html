We acquire and run established UK service businesses.

We work directly with owners and focus on continuity, reputation, and a smooth transition. Our approach is practical and long-term: maintain what works, improve operations over time, and protect customer and staff relationships.

What we look for

  • Well-established service businesses with consistent demand.
  • Strong reputation built over many years.
  • Clear operations and sensible handover potential.
  • Opportunities for incremental, non-disruptive improvement.

What you can expect from us

  • Direct, confidential owner-to-owner discussions.
  • Respect for the business you’ve built and your team.
  • Practical transition planning and handover.
  • Focus on operational continuity and incremental improvements.

What this means for owners

Our approach is designed to preserve what you’ve built. Day-to-day service delivery, customer relationships, and staff continuity matter. Where changes are made, they are measured, practical, and focused on reducing reliance on any one individual over time.

Our process

  • Introductory conversation
  • High-level review of the business
  • Site visit and operational walkthrough
  • Offer and transition planning
