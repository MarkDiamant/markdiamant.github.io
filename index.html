We acquire and run established UK service businesses.

We work directly with owners and focus on continuity, reputation, and a smooth transition. Our approach is practical and long-term: maintain what works, improve operations over time, and protect relationships.

Contact us How we work

What we look for

  • Established service businesses with consistent demand.
  • Strong reputation and long trading history.
  • Clear operations and sensible handover potential.
  • Repeatable work and durable customer relationships.

What you can expect from us

  • Direct, confidential conversations.
  • Respect for what you’ve built and the people involved.
  • Clear questions and straightforward next steps.
  • Openness to phased transitions where helpful.

How we operate

We are operators rather than brokers. Where we proceed, we prioritise a careful handover, keep the existing name and reputation intact, and focus on practical improvements: documentation, scheduling, customer communication, and reducing single-person dependency over time.

Our process

  • Introductory call
  • High-level numbers review
  • Site visit and operational walkthrough
  • Offer and transition plan

Focus

  • Service-led businesses
  • Practical operations
  • Long-term continuity
